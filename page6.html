BEEF AND ROASTED POTATOS
INSTRUCTIONS:

Set the oven to 400°F/200°C

Peel and cut the potatoes into wedges. Slice the pepper and onion.

Add the potatoes on a sheet pan.

Drizzle 1 tbsp of oil on top, season with half the amount of salt and pepper.

Place in the middle of the oven and roast for 30 minutes.

Add oil to a frying pan and cook the onion, sliced peppers, and garlic on medium-high heat. Remove the mixture from the frying pan and turn the heat up.

Cook the steaks the way you like them: rare, medium, or well done.
Ready to serve.

TUNA AND PASTA SALAD
INSTRUCTIONS:

Cook the pasta according to package instructions, rinse until cold in water.

Boil the eggs to your preferred texture (we think seven minutes in boiling water is perfect!)

Let the peas thaw, then mix in spinach, capers, olive oil, salt and pepper.

Top the salad with tuna, egg, basil and some extra salt and pepper if needed.
