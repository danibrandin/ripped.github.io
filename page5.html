OATMEAL BOWL
INSTRUCTONS:

Chop the apples and the bananas.

Add the coconut oil to a frying pan. Add the fruit mixture and coconut, cook for 5 minutes on medium heat, and season with cinnamon and a pinch of salt.

Meanwhile, add the oats and water to a saucepan and bring to boil; let the porridge simmer for 2-3 minutes and then whisk.

Add the fruit compote on the top of the porridge and serve with milk.

ROASTED CHICKEN WITH VEGGIES
INSTRUCTONS:

Preheat the oven to 425°F/225°C.

Season the chicken with salt, thyme and oregano

Cut the potato into wedges or cubes and place in an ovenproof dish.

Drizzle with oil, sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pepper.

Bake in the oven for 15 minutes, then add the chicken and broccoli.

Cook for another 15 minutes or until done.

Shred the cucumber and mix with yogurt, salt, and pepper and serve!
